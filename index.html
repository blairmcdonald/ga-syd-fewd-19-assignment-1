Coding on Saturdays

Coding on Saturdays is a front-end development experiment by Blair McDonald. Over the course of 10 weeks, he will undertake lessons in the art of HTML, CSS and Javascript, and document his results through this site. This experiment currently runs from 15 August to 17 October 2015 and is co-ordinated by General Assembly in Sydney.
